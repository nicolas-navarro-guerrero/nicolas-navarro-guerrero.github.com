I will be supporting the <a target="_blank" href="https://aistis.knu.ua/">International Scientific and Practical Conference “Applied Information Systems and Technologies in the Digital Society” (AISTDS)</a> as a member of the program committee. The conference is organized by the Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv and held online via Zoom on October 1, 2026.

<!--more-->
The conference brings together researchers working on applied information systems and technologies across the digital economy, transport, mechanical engineering, agriculture, finance, banking, energy, medicine, education, science, engineering, information security, infocommunication networks, and the legal and economic aspects of digital transformation.
