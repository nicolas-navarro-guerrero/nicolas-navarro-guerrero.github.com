The inaugural KnowledgeMove conference, organized by hannoverimpuls in cooperation with Leibniz Universität Hannover and Hochschule Hannover, successfully brought together around 200 participants from industry, academia, startups, and policy under the theme “From Knowledge to Impact.”

Representing our research team, Wadhah Zai El Amri exhibited, including live demos, recent advances in tactile perception, highlighting results from the Vibrosense project. The event provided an excellent platform to exchange ideas on technology translation, interdisciplinary collaboration, and the role of startups in advancing intelligent, sensor-based systems for the next generation of human–robot interaction.
