I would like to sincerely thank Prof. Yukie Nagai and the <a href="https://developmental-robotics.jp/en/home/" target="_blank">Cognitive Developmental Robotics Lab at the International Research Center for Neurointelligence</a>, The University of Tokyo, for the warm hospitality and the inspiring research visit. During my stay, I had the opportunity to engage in insightful discussions with lab members and to exchange ideas on current challenges and future directions in tactile perception, embodied robot learning, human-robot interaction, and cognitive development.

<!--more-->
I also had the chance to present my research at the Friday seminar on 14 November 2025, where I presented our work on tactile perception, including the projects ACROSS, Vibrosense, and ROMEO, in the presentation titled “Towards Dexterous Manipulation: From Teleoperation to Autonomous Learning.”
