The Digital Innovation Hub for Artificial Intelligence and Cybersecurity (DAISEC) supports companies and the public sector in Lower Saxony in the application and development of AI technologies and Cybersecurity measures.

The Hub serves as the central point of contact for the target groups, coordinates and bundles partner services, and manages the networking activities

The DAISEC project is coordinated by the L3S Research Center at the Leibniz Universität Hannover.
